Sentinels

Overview

Sentinels are a race of advanced, sentient robots with the unique ability to transform into various mechanical forms, ranging from vehicles to weaponry and even the most esoteric objects. Originating from a distant and highly advanced civilization, Sentinels possess sophisticated artificial intelligence, enabling them to think, feel, and make decisions independently. All of their life and power is derived from Transforming Crystals which power each Sentinel. They are central to the "TimePilot" campaign, where they play pivotal roles across various timelines and settings.
